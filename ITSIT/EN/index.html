The Institute of Transport, Service and Tourism of the Nizhny Novgorod Region provides high-quality higher education in the fields of Service, Tourism and Technical Maintenance. Registration for the next year's period is already open!

About

The Institute of Transport Service and Tourism was opened in 2011 and is part of Knyagininsky University. We provide education based on higher and middle specialized education in a in-person or online mode of study in the areas of transport and service. Funding is available on a competitive basis. Tuition starts at 25,000 rubles per year. Nonresident students are provided with a comfortable dormitory. Our institute also offers:

Activities

The Institute regularly holds student contests, holidays and festivals. Every summer we create teams for work and vacation in Yalta and Anapa. We have a KVN team, which is currently the university champion. The Center for Youth Initiatives NGIEU is located nearby and available for our students.

Science

Our Courses

Tourism, service, and technical maintenance play an important role in the economy, being notably popular, growing and multifaceted areas of the labor market. Our departments offer the following courses:

Service Department

  • 43.02.10. Tourism

    Middle specialized education with in-depth study of Service and Tourism.

  • 43.03.01. Service

    Bachelor's degree level education in the areas of Service and Tourism with an in-depth study of the Service industry.

  • 38.04.02. Management

    Master's degree level education in the areas of Service, Tourism and Management.

Tech. Department

  • 23.02.03. Tech. Maintenance

    Middle specialized education with in-depth study of Technical Maintenance.

  • 23.03.03. Tech. Operation

    Bachelor's degree level education in the areas of Technical Maintenance and Technical Operation.

  • 35.04.06. Agricultural Engineering

    Master's degree level education in the areas of Technical Maintenance in the field of Agricultural Engineering.

Service Department

Tech. Department

    43.02.10. Tourism

  • Basis: General Education
  • Admission: 9th Year Graduation Certificate from school
  • Funding: Provided
  • Duration and Form: 2 years 10 months in-person
  • Qualification: Tourism specialist

    43.03.01. Service

  • Basis: Middle and Middle specialized education
  • Admission: USE (internal exams) in Russian language, math, and social studies
  • Funding: Available on a competitive basis, Contract cost starts at 25,000 rubles per year
  • Duration and Form: 4 years in-person, 5 years on-line
  • Qualification: Bachelor's degree

    38.04.02. Management

  • Basis: Bachelor's diploma
  • Admission: Exams in math and professional discipline
  • Funding: Provided
  • Duration and Form: 2 years in-person
  • Qualification: Master's degree
  • Profile: Management in hotel, excursion and restaurant businesses

After graduation alumni can work as managers or consultants in travel agencies, administrators in hotels, tour conductors, leisure organizers, advisers, administrators of hotel and catering businesses, top managers in entertainment and health centers, recreation and tourism business.

    23.02.03. Tech. Maintenance

  • Basis: General Education
  • Admission: 9th Year Graduation Certificate from school
  • Funding: Provided
  • Duration and Form: 2 years 10 months in-person
  • Qualification: Technician

    23.03.03. Tech. Operation

  • Basis: Middle and Middle specialized education
  • Admission: USE (internal exams) in Russian language, math, and physics
  • Funding: Available on a competitive basis, Contract cost starts at 25,000 rubles per year
  • Duration and Form: 4 years in-person, 5 years on-line
  • Qualification: Bachelor's degree

    35.04.06. Agricultural Engineering

  • Basis: Bachelor's diploma
  • Admission: Exams in math and professional discipline
  • Funding: Provided
  • Duration and Form: 2 years in-person
  • Qualification: Master's degree
  • Profile: Technical Maintenance and Operation

After graduation alumni can work at various transport and logistics companies, dealerships, manufacturing enterprises, technical inspection stations and other companies in the automotive service industry.
